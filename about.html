About Marta Studio

Marta Studio is a creative studio focused on brand identity, packaging, and design that connects with people.

Services

  • Brand Design – logos, typography, identity systems
  • Packaging Design – product packaging, labels, mockups
  • Print Production – stationery, brochures, posters
  • Digital Assets – presentations, web graphics, ads
  • Social Media Design – templates, campaigns, digital storytelling

Marta Romac, Designer

Marta Romac is the designer behind Marta Studio, with over 5 years of experience in brand identity and packaging design. She is passionate about shaping the visual side of brands, creating designs that connect with people and tell authentic stories.
