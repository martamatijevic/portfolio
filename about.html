About Marta Studio

Marta Studio is a creative studio focused on brand design, packaging, print production, and digital assets. We help brands tell stories through visuals.

Services

  • Brand Design – logos, typography, identity systems
  • Packaging Design – product packaging, labels, mockups
  • Print Production – stationery, brochures, posters
  • Digital Assets – presentations, web graphics, ads
  • Social Media Design – templates, campaigns, digital storytelling

Marta Romac, Designer

The designer behind Marta Studio is Marta Romac, with 5+ years of experience in brand design and visual storytelling. Passionate about creating the visual side of brands.
