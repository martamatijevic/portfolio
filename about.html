About Marta Studio

Marta Studio is a creative design studio focused on brand identity, packaging and visual storytelling. We believe that design should connect with people and tell a meaningful story.

Services

  • Brand Design – logos, typography, identity systems
  • Packaging Design – product packaging, labels, mockups
  • Print Production – stationery, brochures, posters
  • Digital Assets – presentations, web graphics, ads
  • Social Media Design – templates, campaigns, digital storytelling

Marta Romac, Designer

Marta Romac is the designer behind Marta Studio, with 5+ years of experience in crafting brand identities and visual storytelling. She is passionate about shaping the visual side of brands and creating meaningful connections through design.
