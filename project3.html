Aurea fest Požega

Brand Design / Print Production

From 2018 to 2021, we were responsible for the visual identity of the well-known Slavonian festival in Požega. The former name, Aurea Fest Požega, was incorporated into the new logo we designed. We carefully developed artist promotions in both digital and print formats, as well as CD design, program booklets, T-shirts, accreditations and many more. The images present selected visual elements from the festival’s 60th anniversary, which took place in 2019.
